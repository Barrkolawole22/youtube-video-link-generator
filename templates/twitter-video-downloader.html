Frequently Asked Questions - Twitter Video Downloader

Can I download private Twitter videos?

No, our downloader only works with publicly available Twitter videos. Private videos or content from protected accounts cannot be downloaded as they require authentication.

What's the highest quality I can download Twitter videos in?

You can download Twitter videos in the highest quality available on the platform, which is typically up to 720p. Some newer content may be available in 1080p quality.

Why do some Twitter videos have no sound?

Some videos on Twitter, especially GIFs, are uploaded without audio. If the original content has no sound, the downloaded version will also be silent. This is not an issue with our downloader.

Can I download videos from the new X.com domain?

Yes, our downloader supports both twitter.com and x.com domains. The rebranding of Twitter to X doesn't affect our service's functionality.
