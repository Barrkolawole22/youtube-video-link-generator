PLATFORM TIPS

Tips for Each Platform

YouTube

For YouTube Shorts, use the regular YouTube URL. You can also add "save" to the beginning of YouTube URLs:

https://youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ
https://saveyoutube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ

Instagram

For Instagram, make sure you're copying the direct post URL, not a story URL. For Reels, get the URL from the share button.

Facebook

For Facebook videos, copy the URL from the address bar while viewing the video. For Reels, get the link via the share button.

Twitter/X

For Twitter, copy the link to the individual tweet that contains the video. Works with both twitter.com and x.com domains.
